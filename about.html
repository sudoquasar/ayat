About ayaत

Preserving and celebrating the rich heritage of Indian classical music

Our Story

ayaत was born from a deep passion for Indian classical music and a desire to recreate the intimate, authentic experience of traditional baithak sessions. The word "ayat" signifies verses and poetry, reflecting the lyrical beauty and depth of classical music.

Founded in Bangalore, we recognized a growing community of classical music enthusiasts who craved more than just concerts—they sought connection, intimacy, and the authentic baithak experience where music is not just heard but felt and lived.

What is a Baithak?

A baithak is a traditional, intimate gathering where musicians perform in close proximity to the audience. Unlike formal concerts, baithaks foster a personal connection between the artist and listeners, creating an atmosphere where music becomes a shared experience.

In the baithak tradition, audiences sit on the floor, often in someone's home, creating a warm, welcoming environment. This setting allows for spontaneity, interaction, and a deeper appreciation of the nuances of classical music.

Our Values

Authenticity

We stay true to the traditional baithak format while making it accessible to contemporary audiences.

Excellence

We curate performances by accomplished artists who embody the highest standards of classical music.

Community

We foster a welcoming space where music lovers can connect, share, and grow together.

Intimacy

We maintain small, intimate gatherings to preserve the essence of the baithak experience.

Our Mission

To create a vibrant ecosystem for Indian classical music in Bangalore, where traditions are honored, artists are celebrated, and audiences discover the transformative power of live music in intimate settings. We believe in making classical music accessible without compromising its authenticity, nurturing both seasoned connoisseurs and curious newcomers.
