Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Ayat Baithaks

Booking & Tickets

How do I book tickets for an event?

Visit our Baithaks page to view all upcoming events. Click on "Book Your Seat" button for the event you wish to attend. You'll need to make payment via UPI first, then complete the registration form with your details and UPI transaction ID.

What payment methods do you accept?

We currently accept payments via UPI (Unified Payments Interface). After clicking the UPI payment link, you can pay using any UPI app like Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or directly through your bank's UPI service.

How many tickets can I book at once?

You can book up to 10 tickets per booking, subject to availability. Each ticket includes entry, seating, and refreshments for one person.

Will I receive a confirmation?

Yes! After completing your booking, you'll receive a confirmation email with all event details, venue information, and your booking reference. Please save this email and present it at the venue if requested.

Can I cancel or modify my booking?

Please contact us at ayataaraindia@gmail.com or call +91-7019250861 as soon as possible if you need to cancel or modify your booking. Cancellation and refund policies will be communicated based on the timing of your request.

Event Details

What is the venue capacity?

We maintain intimate settings with limited seating, typically ranging from 20 to 40 people. This ensures an authentic baithak experience where everyone can connect closely with the artist and the music.

Where are the events held?

Our baithaks are held at various intimate venues across Bangalore. The exact venue address is shared in your booking confirmation email, typically 2-3 days before the event. This helps us maintain the personal and exclusive nature of our gatherings.

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive 15-20 minutes before the scheduled start time. This allows you to settle in, enjoy some refreshments, and soak in the ambiance before the performance begins. Latecomers may have to wait for an appropriate break before entering to avoid disturbing the performance.

How long do the events typically last?

Most baithak sessions last between 2 to 3 hours, including intervals. The duration may vary depending on the artist and the format of the performance. We recommend keeping your evening free to fully immerse yourself in the experience.

Baithak Experience

What is a baithak?

A baithak is a traditional, intimate gathering where musicians perform in close proximity to the audience. Unlike formal concerts in large auditoriums, baithaks foster a personal connection between the artist and listeners. Guests typically sit on the floor in a cozy setting, creating an atmosphere where music becomes a shared, immersive experience.

What should I wear?

We recommend comfortable, modest attire suitable for sitting on the floor. Traditional Indian wear is welcome but not mandatory. Avoid formal shoes as you'll need to remove them before entering the performance area. Think comfortable and respectful.

Are refreshments provided?

Yes! We provide curated refreshments including tea, coffee, and light snacks. When booking, you can specify your food preferences (vegetarian, non-vegetarian, vegan, or no food). We strive to accommodate special dietary requirements – just mention them in the special requests section of the booking form.

Can I bring children?

Children who can sit quietly and appreciate classical music for extended periods are welcome. However, given the intimate nature and duration of our performances, we recommend bringing children aged 10 and above who have an interest in music. Please book a separate ticket for each child attending.

Is photography or recording allowed?

Photography and recording policies vary by event and artist preference. We'll inform you of specific guidelines in the booking confirmation email. Generally, we encourage you to be fully present in the moment rather than behind a camera or phone screen.

What if I've never attended a classical music event before?

Everyone is welcome, regardless of their knowledge of classical music! Our baithaks are perfect for both seasoned connoisseurs and curious newcomers. The intimate setting allows you to experience the music in a way that's accessible and moving. We often provide brief introductions to help everyone appreciate the performance better.
